About Books for DC

The booksfordc browser extension lets you easily determine the availability of books, ebooks, and audiobooks at the Washington, DC Public Library as you browse Amazon, Goodreads, and Barnes & Noble.

For in-depth instructions on setting up and using this extension, please visit our homepage.

Support the Project

All booksfordc tools are made available at no cost to users, but you can show your support for this community project by making a contribution. Think of all the money it saves you!
