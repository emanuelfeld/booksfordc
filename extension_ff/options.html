about booksfordc

The booksfordc browser extension lets you easily determine the availability of books, ebooks, and audiobooks at the DC Public Library as you browse Amazon, Goodreads, and Barnes & Noble.

support the project

All booksfordc tools are made available at no cost to users, but you can show your support for this community project by making a voluntary contribution. Think of all the money it saves you!

preferences

You can change these options at any time from the Chrome extensions menu or by clicking on the booksfordc toolbar icon.
