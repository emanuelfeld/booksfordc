A Yearly Update from Books for DC

Season's greetings!

Books for DC held up well in 2017 after a complete rewrite of its codebase. The @booksfordc Twitter feed was also recently spruced up. We now include cover photos for each of the DC Public Library's newly added books. Check it out and follow us here.

Find any great library books using Books for DC? We'd love to hear about it. Let us know by Twitter or email.

Our browser extension counts 600 regular users, a number we hope to grow in 2018. Why not all 690k residents? You can help us get there by:

Sharing Books for DC in your social networks: Twitter, Facebook, and offline.

Leaving us a 5 ★ review in the Chrome store.

Submitting bug reports.

We also have brand new canvas tote bags for sale! Get one to show off your bibliophilic bonafides, carry your huge library haul, and spread the word about Books for DC on the District's streets.
